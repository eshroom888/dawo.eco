## Executive Summary

Transform DAWO Biohack from a manually-operated one-man e-commerce business into an AI-first company where specialized AI agents handle content creation, B2B sales outreach, and competitor compliance monitoring. This project leverages the existing IMAGO.ECO platform infrastructure to deploy Google ADK-based agents that automate time-consuming operational tasks while maintaining EU regulatory compliance.

**Core Vision**: "AI employees" that work 24/7, freeing the human operator to focus on strategy, relationships, and growth.

## 5. Regulatory Environment (CRITICAL)

### 5.1 EU Novel Foods Regulation (EU 2015/2283)

DAWO's products must comply with Novel Foods regulations:

| Product Form | Status | Compliance |
|--------------|--------|------------|
| Fruiting body extracts | NOT Novel | ✅ Compliant |
| Powder/extract supplements | NOT Novel | ✅ Compliant |
| Mycelium powder | NOVEL | ❌ Requires EFSA authorization |
| Non-supplement uses | NOVEL | ❌ Requires authorization |

**DAWO Status**: Ultrasonic water extracts from fruiting bodies for supplement use - COMPLIANT

### 5.2 EU Health Claims Regulation (EC 1924/2006)

**PROHIBITED Claims**:
- Disease prevention ("prevents dementia")
- Therapeutic/medicinal ("treats anxiety")
- Unapproved health claims
- Stronger language than EU-authorized wording

**ALLOWED Claims** (if in EU Register):
- Function claims about normal bodily functions
- Must use wording "as close as possible" to authorized claims
- Example: "Vitamin D contributes to the maintenance of normal bones"

**Content Agent Requirements**:
1. Only use claims from EU Register of Health Claims
2. Flag any content suggesting disease treatment/prevention
3. Avoid "stronger" language than authorized wording
4. Keep lifestyle layer separate from health claims layer

### 5.3 Regulatory Sources

- [EU Register of Health Claims](https://food.ec.europa.eu/food-safety/labelling-and-nutrition/nutrition-and-health-claims/health-claims_en)

## 8. Agent Specifications

### 8.1 Content Agent

**Purpose**: Generate EU-compliant marketing content for social media, email, and blog.

**Capabilities**:
- Fetch product data from Shopify via MCP
- Generate social media posts (Instagram, LinkedIn)
- Create Klaviyo email campaign drafts
- Produce blog post outlines for SEO
- Check all content against EU compliance rules
- Queue drafts for human approval

**Brand Voice Guidelines**:
- **Outer Layer**: Simple, lifestyle/wellness focused, approachable
- **Inner Layer**: Scientific/educational depth for engaged users
- **Local Identity**: Oslo roots, Nordic nature, sustainability
- **Compliance**: Never cross into medicinal/therapeutic claims

**Tools Required**:
- `fetch_shopify_products()` - Get product data
- `check_eu_compliance()` - Validate claims
- `draft_social_post()` - Generate content
- `notify_discord()` - Alert for approval

**Output**: Draft content in approval queue with compliance status

### 8.2 Sales Agent

**Purpose**: Research and qualify B2B leads, generate personalized outreach drafts.

**Target Markets** (Priority Order):
1. Health food stores (Sunkost, Life, local organic shops)
2. Gyms and fitness centers
3. Cafes and juice bars
4. International distributors (future)

**Capabilities**:
- Research Norwegian health food stores
- Enrich leads with contact info, decision makers
- Generate personalized outreach emails
- Track outreach status and responses
- Schedule follow-up reminders
- Communicate with Content Agent via A2A

**Tools Required**:
- `search_businesses()` - Find potential leads
- `enrich_lead()` - Get contact details
- `draft_outreach()` - Create personalized email
- `track_lead_status()` - Update CRM data
- `notify_discord()` - Alert for new qualified leads

**Output**: Qualified leads with outreach drafts ready for approval

### 8.3 CleanMarket Agent (Compliance Monitor)

**Purpose**: Monitor competitor marketing for EU regulation violations.

**What to Monitor**:

| Violation Type | EU Regulation | Example |
|----------------|---------------|---------|
| Unapproved health claims | EC 1924/2006 | "Cures brain fog" |
| Medicinal claims | EC 1924/2006 | "Treats anxiety" |
| Disease prevention | EC 1924/2006 | "Prevents dementia" |
| Novel food violations | EU 2015/2283 | Unauthorized mycelium |
| Misleading advertising | EU 2005/29 | False organic claims |

**Capabilities**:
- Scrape competitor websites (public pages only)
- Extract health claims from product descriptions
- Check claims against EU Health Claims Register
- Flag potential violations with evidence
- Store violations in database with timestamps
- Generate weekly compliance reports
- Optionally: Anonymous reporting to Mattilsynet

**Legal Constraints**:
- Public information only
- No hacking or intrusion
- Standard web requests only
- Evidence-based reporting only
- No defamation

**Tools Required**:
- `scrape_website()` - Fetch competitor pages
- `extract_claims()` - Parse health claims
- `check_compliance_db()` - Validate against EU register
- `store_violation()` - Log to database
- `generate_report()` - Weekly summary
- `notify_discord()` - Alert on serious violations

**Output**: Weekly compliance report, violation database, competitive intelligence

### 8.4 Support Agent (Future Phase)

**Purpose**: Handle customer inquiries with two-tier escalation model.

**Tier 1 (AI Handles)**:
- Product information questions
- Order status inquiries
- Shipping information
- FAQ responses
- Basic troubleshooting

**Tier 2 (Human Escalation)**:
- Complaints and issues
- Complex questions
- Custom requests
- Relationship-building conversations

**Implementation**: Future phase after core agents are operational
